Football (soccer)

  1. A game played between 2 teams of 11 players

  2. The goal is to score by kicking a ball into the others team net.

  3. The team with the most goals is crowned the winner

  4. Each match last 90 minutes split into 2 halves of 45mins

  5. The most popular sport with over 1 billion watchers

  6. Around 250 million players in over 200 countries
